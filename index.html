Methodology

Our system enables automated evaluation and generation of medical image reports through a modular visual interface. (1) In the evaluation phase, users log in and complete a structured survey based on medical images, which are dynamically rendered from uploaded datasets. These images—supporting formats like DICOM, PNG, and NIfTI—are displayed with interactive tools such as zoom, pan, rotation, and contrast adjustment to enhance visual comprehension. (2) In the generation phase, users upload one or more medical images, which are immediately previewed and analyzed. The system synthesizes preliminary reports by integrating image metadata and visual features. Throughout both phases, the frontend facilitates seamless interaction, while backend pipelines prepare the data for further clinical processing, annotation, or validation.
